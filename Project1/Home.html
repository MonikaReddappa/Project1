Introduction

A technological assistance is required to aid the blind in walking fast and safely to their destination, specifically assistance with providing warning when they are about to hit an obstacle and want to step down the curb. In addition, it can be hard to know when you should cross the street. 
A blind pedestrian can make better decisions using technology by detecting the color of the stoplight and the movements surrounding the stoplight. Vision impairment makes it difficult for people to find objects in front of them. 
It is my proposal to develop an application that can detect objects on the floor so that people can commute on a daily basis.
